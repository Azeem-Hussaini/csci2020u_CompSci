The University of Ontario Institute of Technology, corporately branded as Ontario Tech University or Ontario Tech,[5] is a public research university located in Oshawa, Ontario, Canada.[6] Ontario Tech's main campus is located on approximately 400 acres (160 ha) of land in the northern part of Oshawa. It operates a secondary campus in the downtown region of Oshawa.

The school founded in 2002 via the University of Ontario Institute of Technology Act passed by the Government of Ontario.[7]

Within 15 years of its founding Ontario Tech quickly earned a reputation for computer science, ranking among the top 10 programs in Canada and 4th in Ontario
